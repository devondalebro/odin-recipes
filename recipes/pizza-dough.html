Pizza dough

Easy homemade pizza dough recipe so you don't have to wait for th dough to rise!

Ingredients

  • Yeast: active dry yeast so the pizza dough rises
  • Sugar: sugar and warm water will activate the yeast
  • Bread flour: creates chiewer results than normal flour
  • Olive oil: adds colour and fllavour and also a barrier between the oil and water
  • Salt: adds flavour, strengthens the gluten, slows down fermentation

Steps

  1. Roll out the pizza dough
  2. Let the dough come to room temperature
  3. Prepare your surface with oil
  4. Shape the dough
