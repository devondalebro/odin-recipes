Mac and Cheese

This is a simple mac and cheese recipe to easily make at home.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni: of course this is the foundation of every mac and cheese meal
  • Butter and flour: this is for the roux for the cheese sauce and also two tablespoosn of butter for the topping
  • Milk: obviously milk for the richest flavour and texture
  • Seasonings: salt and pepper for the sauce and paprika for the topping
  • Bread crumbs: you can easily make this at home

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles, drain them and then transfer to a prepared baking dish.
  2. Make and cheese sauce and pour it over the noodles, then stir.
  3. Make the topping, spread it over the macaroni and cheese, and sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake the mac and cheese until the topping is golden brown.
