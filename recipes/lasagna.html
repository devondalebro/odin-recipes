Lasagna

Here is a little sophisticated lasagna recipe that takes a little work, but is totally worth it!

Ingredients

  • Meat: a nice combination is sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic to add flavour to the meat
  • Tomato stuff: can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce and two cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar: two tablespoons will add some sweetness and enhance the flavour of the sauce
  • Spices and seasonings: fresh parsley , dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds and black pepper for extra flavour
  • Lasagna noodles: just some stored bought lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses: parmesan, mozzarrella, ricotta cheese
  • Egg: binds the ricotta so that it doesn't ooze out the lasagna when you cut it

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 2 hrs 30 mins
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
